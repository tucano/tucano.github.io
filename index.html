Old, strange projects from the past

FancyGene was a tool to generate PDF and PNG images of genes/domains and include them in presentations and papers
Splicy aka Gene Chip splice machine was a tool to store and display original data relatives to single probes mapping over Exons.
NemaFootPrinter aka The Comparative Nematodes gene browsers was a tool to compare genomes in nematodes
flowFit was a Bioconductor package to estimate proliferation in cell-tracking dye studies
