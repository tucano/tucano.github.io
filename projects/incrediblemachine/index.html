The Incredible Machine#

A mechanism designed in a deliberately complex way to do simple or awesome tasks. Loosely based on the cartoons by Rube Goldberg, who used his engineering skills to create machines of this kind in many of his works. In this case, Analog and Digital combine to give this “machine” the astonishing feature of Perpetual Motion!!
